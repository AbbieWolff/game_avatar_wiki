Social Consequences

Definition
Any action taken does not exist in the vacuum, so it is subject to social consequences. Generally, a negative consequence happens when people break *social norms*. Both social norms and subsequent social consequences depend on the community.

This is unsurprisingly similar to the real world; after all, virtual communities are simply way of interacting(Adrian).
