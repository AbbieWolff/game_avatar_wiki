Uses for Virtual Reality & Augmented Reality

As numerous companies are currently developing Virtual and Augmented Reality technology products (such as Microsoft, Valve, and Oculus) and social scientists studying virtual reality can attest, the possible uses for virtual reality and augmented reality are as broad as the possibilities for real life activities.

Some possible uses of this technology are already being undertaken: including improving quality of life for everyone from soldiers to kids and may even helping to modify players' behavior.
Virtual Reality
    Virtual reality applications currently in use include:
  • Virtual therapy--immersing patients with phobias and veterans with PTSD in virtual environments to help them confront and heal from trauma and fear. 1
  • Exploring distant environments, like Mars, for scientific research. 2
  • Realistic, interactive 3D military training simulations. ?
Augmented Reality
    Similarly, augmented reality is being used to:
  • Help with navigation ?
  • Provide smart helmets to soldiers 3
  • Assist doctors, surgeons, and other medical personnel with surgery 4
