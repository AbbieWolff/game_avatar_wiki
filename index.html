Identity Exploration via Video Game Avatars

Avatar:
An icon or figure representing a particular person in computer games, Internet forums, etc. ?
Relevant virtual worlds have three characteristics: 1
  • Real time conversation
  • Fully customizable virtual self-presentations (avatars)
  • A representative world, potentially 3D
Usually, people spend time in virtual worlds for a combination of any of these reasons: 1
  • Build personal relationships
  • Earn money
  • Escape emotionally or mentally from their current surroundings; be immersed in another world
  • Learn
